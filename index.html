The Credit Broken Wing Butterfly (BWB)

I love the Credit BWB. Itâ€™s a high-probability play that removes all risk from one side of the market and can be set up using calls or puts to express neutral, bearish, or bullish views.

The Strategy Mechanics

  • The "No-Risk" Side: Because the trade is opened for a net credit, if the market moves away from your spread, you simply keep the initial credit. There is zero risk in that direction.

The "Harvesting" Technique

The real power of the BWB lies in the management phase. If the trade goes my way, I "harvest" the broken wing by rolling it in toward the short strikes.

The "Lotto" Transformation: If the cost of that roll is less than my initial entry credit, the trade becomes a risk-free lottery ticket. At that point, the worst-case scenario is a small guaranteed profit, while hitting the short strikes at expiration delivers a huge payday.
