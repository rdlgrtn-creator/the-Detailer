40-Year Monte Carlo Retirement Simulator

Model Overview

This advanced Excel model uses a stochastic (randomized) approach to forecast retirement success. While standard calculators assume a constant 7% return, this engine runs 1,000 parallel simulations to account for market volatility and the "Sequence of Returns" risk.

1. The "Engine" (Logic Flow)

The model utilizes modern Excel functional programming:

  • MAP: Triggers the 1,000-life dataset.
  • REDUCE: Manages the 40-year compounding timeline.
  • LET: Optimizes complex market and tax variables.

2. Core Financial Logic

Feature Technical Execution
Market Volatility Random returns pulled from a Normal Distribution based on custom Mean/Std Dev.
Spending Guardrails Automatic 20% spending reduction if Total Wealth drops below $1.1M.
Drawdown Order Prioritizes Cash, then Taxable Brokerage, then Tax-Advantaged accounts.
