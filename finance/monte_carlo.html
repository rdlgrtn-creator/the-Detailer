40-Year Monte Carlo Retirement Simulator

This model uses a stochastic approach to forecast retirement success. Instead of assuming a static return, it simulates 1,000 different market paths to show the probability of your portfolio surviving 40 years.

1. Technical Model Overview

The "Engine" is built using advanced Excel logic:

  • MAP Function: Used to trigger the 1,000 randomized "parallel lives."
  • REDUCE Function: Processes the 40-year recursive timeline.
  • Sequence of Returns: Models the risk of a market crash occurring early in retirement.
