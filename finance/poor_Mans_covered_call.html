the detailer | The Poor Man’s Covered Call (PMCC)

A capital-efficient alternative to owning 100 shares of stock outright.

How It Works
  • Buy the "Synthetic" Stock: A deep ITM call (80 Delta) in a long-term expiration cycle.
  • Sell the "Rent": A near-term OTM call (30 Delta) to collect premium.
Avoiding the "Cardinal Sin"
Never exercise your long call early. By exercising, you throw away the extrinsic (time) value. Instead, sell the long call and use the cash to buy back the short shares.
Real-World Example: RDDT (Feb 9, 2026)

Stock Price: $141

Component Detail Metric
Long Call $110 Strike (May 15 Exp) 80 Delta
Short Call $165 Strike (Mar 20 Exp) 30 Delta
Net Debit Paid $34.38 Total Risk ($3,438)

Max Profit: (165 - 110) - 34.38 = $20.62 ($2,062)

The "Detailer" Edge

Buying 100 shares of RDDT costs $14,100. This PMCC controls the same 100 shares for $3,438—a 75% reduction in capital requirements.
