Randall's Island: My New Favorite 6-Mile Loop

Randall's Island has become my 2nd favorite place to run. Here is usually where I start and finish my runs totaling about 6 miles.

First, get on the Triborough Bridge pedestrian walkway near 1st Avenue and 125th Street to get on the island. You can do a clockwise and counter-clockwise loop around the entire island.

To finish, get on the pedestrian bridge on the east (103rd Street), run south to Carl Schurz Park at 91st Street, do some push-ups, and end the run.
